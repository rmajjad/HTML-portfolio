About My University

Palestine Technical University - Kadoorie

Palestine Technical University - Kadoorie (PTUK) is a prominent higher education institution in Palestine. Established in 1930 as a technical and agricultural school, it has grown to become a full-fledged university offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. PTUK is located in Tulkarm, with additional branches in Ramallah and Hebron.

The university is known for its emphasis on technical and vocational education, aiming to equip students with practical skills and knowledge that meet the needs of the local and regional job markets. PTUK offers programs in various fields including engineering, applied sciences, business, and humanities.

In addition to academic programs, PTUK is involved in research and community service, contributing to the development of Palestinian society. The university collaborates with international institutions and organizations to enhance its educational standards and provide students with opportunities for global exposure.
